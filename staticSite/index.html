Vocalize is an app that uses a Virtual Native Speaker from one of our many languages to give you live feedback on your pronunciation.

Accurate Results

The Vocalize app combines thousands of word samples from various audio sources to create a word archetype. The user's recorded audio is processed against the word archetype returning a score based on correctness.

Multiple Languages

The Vocalize app contains some of the most popular second languages on planet earth for language learners. We are constanly adding more languages we hope to be able to offer every language learner a Virtual Native Speaker.

Multiple platforms

We here at Vocalize feel that language learning should not have limits which is why we offer our one of a kind app on mobile and desktop platforms. If you are waiting on the bus or have some downtime in your home office you can use vocalize.
