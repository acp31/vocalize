Vocalize is an app that uses a Virtual Native Speaker from one of our many languages to give you live feedback on your pronunciation.

Accurate Results

The Vocalize app combines thousands of word samples from various audio sources to create a word archetype. The user's recorded audio is processed against the word archetype returning a score based on correctness.

How it works!

How can we meaningfully gauge and practice the accuracy of pronunciation and accent?

Data Scraping

Get Spoken Audio from Youtube

Transcribe Audio Content with Watson Speech to Text

Use Transcription to Process Audio into Word Samples

Processing: Averaging

Compile multiple versions of the word

Combine those versions into one avgerage word

This average word becomes the new word archtype

Processing: Comparison

Take the users recorded audio

compare the user recording with the archtype

A score is retured which is based on the closenes of the user's recorded audio and the archtype
